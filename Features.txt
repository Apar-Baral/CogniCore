| # | Feature | What It Does | Why Hermes Needs It |
|---|---|---|---|

| 11 | **Auto-Corrector** | Generates corrected code when hallucinations are detected. Confidence-scored: HIGH auto-apply, MEDIUM suggest, LOW flag. Produces unified diffs | Blocking without fixing frustrates users. This provides the fix, not just the error |
| 12 | **Validation Statistics** | Tracks total validations, hallucination rate by category, auto-correction success rate, and average validation time | Enables trend analysis and proves the shield's value |
